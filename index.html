JPI Education Courses

At JPI Education we focus on the strengths and weaknesses of each student to help them reach their full potential. We use our understanding of overseas education systems to simulate the thinking and teaching of Australian Schools. This helps students progress from English as a Foreign Language to English as a Second Language (Essential English), and prepare for the AEAS test. Through our Campus English course, we foster the language and study skills required for Higher School Certificate success.
